About Me

Uyanda Franci Shabalala Mahlalempini was born in the sunny & ever green city Durban in KwaZulu Natal South Africa. Uyanda is a drama and media graduate from City Varsity School of creative arts & media. She majored in acting and discovered her passion and talent for TV and Radio Broadcasting during her years of studying and was advised to become a broadcaster. She is a programme director, television and radio broadcaster, dancer, and voice-over artist. She has experience in TV presenting and was given the platform to showcase her talent during the My Voice is Power red carpet interviews. She made it to the top 5 in a podcast and presenter search on Reason Media TV on YouTube, and she has experience in programme directing in corporate and entertainment events. She has performed as a dancer/mover at the Joburg theatre in a play called Human Nature directed and choreographed by Pieter Human.
